How this summary works:Consumptive use — the water crops actually consumed, estimated from satellite evapotranspiration (ET) — is the demand. It is met by three supplies: surface-water deliveries, groundwater pumping, and effective rainfall. The columns below show each area's consumptive use beside the supplies that met it; the balance is the supplies minus consumptive use.
