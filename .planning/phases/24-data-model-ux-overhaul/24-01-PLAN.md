<objective>
Redesign the three hardest UX problems in the platform: allocation-optional accounting, surface diversions without water rights, and zone management. Make the platform work for agencies that just track water use without formal allocations or water rights data.

Purpose: 40% of small water systems use zero digital monitoring. Many agencies tracking water use don't have formal allocations or water right permits. The current dashboard breaks (shows negative "Remaining" against zero allocation) and the surface diversions page forces users to start from a water right. This phase removes those barriers.

Output: Allocation-optional dashboard, POD-centric surface diversion views with inline diversion entry, zone management page with map drawing. All with 186+ tests passing.
</objective>
