Reporting / Reports / Shared-supply check

When one well or canal feeds several fields, someone sets each field's share of that water by hand. This page compares each hand-entered share against the share that estimated satellite crop-water use (ET) would imply, and flags any field where the two are far apart — a likely typo worth a look on the source's own page. It only points out where to check; it never changes a split.
