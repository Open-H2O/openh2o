What is a methodology?

A methodology (or calculation plan) is the ordered, configurable chain of steps the platform applies to turn measurements into a defensible billable-groundwater figure for each use area: gross ET, minus effective precipitation, minus surface water deliveries, minus edge cases. Each step below is one operation in that chain — reorder it, enable or disable it, and tune its knobs, then preview a sample parcel to see the effect.
