What this page is for

A laboratory tests water and sends back a file. Every line of that file says where the water was taken from — a particular well, or a particular tap out in the neighbourhood. This page is where you tell the system what those places are, so the lab's results have somewhere to land.

Until a place is listed here, a lab result naming it cannot be filed. The import will report it and skip it rather than guess.

Each place gets a code, built from three parts: the water system's id, the facility it belongs to, and a number or short label you choose. You supply only the last part — the rest is already known.
