OpenH2O

Open-source water accounting for California

A poorly-funded agency buys a frontier AI subscription, points it at this repo and a $15/mo VPS, and the AI stands the platform up. Access is the product, not features. OpenH2O replaces $35K-$75K consulting engagements with open-source infrastructure any Groundwater Sustainability Agency can deploy.

Open and Transparent Water Data Act mandates public data integration
