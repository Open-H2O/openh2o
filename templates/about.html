OpenH2O

Open-source water accounting for California

OpenH2O is an open-source water accounting platform built for Groundwater Sustainability Agencies and water districts. It tracks groundwater extraction, surface water diversions, managed aquifer recharge, and mixed-use accounting in a single system, then generates the state reports agencies are required to file. The entire platform runs on a $15/month virtual private server and can be deployed by an AI coding assistant pointed at this repository. No consulting contract required.
